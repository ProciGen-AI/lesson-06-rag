# Fundamental Resources

Here are some company resources you'll need to be successful as a manager at 37signals. This playbook refers back to these resources in later chapters.

### [37signals Employee Handbook](https://github.com/basecamp/handbook/blob/master/README.md)

Everything you need to know about 37signals company policies in the handbook is here. This is the general handbook for all staff.

### [Designers](https://github.com/basecamp/handbook/blob/master/titles-for-designers.md), [Ops](https://github.com/basecamp/handbook/blob/master/titles-for-ops.md), [Programmers](https://github.com/basecamp/handbook/blob/master/titles-for-programmers.md), [QA](https://github.com/basecamp/handbook/blob/master/titles-for-QA.md), & [Customer Support](https://github.com/basecamp/handbook/blob/master/titles-for-support.md) Career Progression Frameworks

These are the frameworks you use to assess the job performance of your report. You also use these to measure whether your report is performing at a higher level and promotable. Each job function has their own model, and each job is organized by seniority levels. Every framework outlines the Skills, Engagement, and Coachability expected at each level. The performance review you complete annually for your report mirrors these frameworks. You should also refer to these throughout the year to complete quick, high level performance assessment scans.

### For Performance Reviews: [Manager Review Forms](https://github.com/alarowe/manager-playbook/tree/main/assets/manager-evaluations) and [Employee Self-Reflection Forms](https://github.com/alarowe/manager-playbook/tree/main/assets/self-evaluations)

These are reference copies. You and your report will be assigned Jotform surveys when it's time for performance reviews.
